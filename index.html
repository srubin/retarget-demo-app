Music retargeting demo

What is this?

This is a tool to change the length of a music track. First select a track, then choose a name, target duration, and whether you want to force the output to start at the beginning of of the track and end at the end of the track.

What do the results mean?

In addition to the retargeting parameters, the results list shows two things: the location and cost of all the transitions in the new audio track. You can scrub to these areas to listen to the transitions. Low-cost transitions are likely to have imperceptible audio transitions.

Why does it take so long?

Retargeting itself is very fast. The slow parts of this app are doing the pre-analysis of the track, and converting the generated wav to an mp3. If you want to skip the lengthy pre-analysis step, please select one of the pre-analyzed music tracks from the dropdown box.
